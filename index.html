Animal facts that make you smile!

If you’ve ever found yourself wondering why some animals do the things they do, here are some interesting facts to keep you guessing:

  • Dogs only sweat from their feet, and they can discharge heat only by panting.
  • Cats have better memories than dogs. A dog's memory lasts no more than 5 minutes, a cat's can last as long as 16 hours, exceeding even that of monkeys.
  • Egyptians were very fond of dogs, as greyhound appears in Egyptian art.
  • Puffins mate for life. They make their homes on cliff sides and set aside room for their own toilet space.
  • Ferrets are currently the third most popular pet in the US. There are an estimated eight to ten million ferrets in the United States being kept as pets.


Simple things you can do to be a great pet parent!

Here are some more interesting funny facts to learn about your pet animal, that will make your day better:

  1. Pet-Proof Home. As a new pet parent, you have to make sure dangerous items are out of reach for your furry friend.
  2. Create a pet schedule. They need a constant routine, and one that introduces him to you and your new home (to him).
  3. Choose a Quality Diet. Nutritional needs will vary depending on the pet’s life stage. From child to adulthood pet.
  4. Focus on Positive Training for Behavior Issues. Be consistent with training and always keep it positive.
  5. Take your pet to the veterinary regularly. In many ways the veterinarian will be your most trusted advisor.
